$(\xi, \eta)$为联合分布的随机变量，$g(\xi)$为可积随机变量，则：
$$
E\{E[g(\xi)|\eta]\}=E[g(\xi)]
$$

证明：
$$
\begin{aligned}
&E\{E[g(\xi)|\eta]\}\\
=&\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} E[g(\xi)|\eta =y]p_2(y)dy\\
=&\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}g(\xi)p(\xi = x|\eta = y)p_2(y)dxdy\\
=&\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}g(\xi)p(x, y)dxdy\\
=&E[g(\xi)]
\end{aligned}
$$
